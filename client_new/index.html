Jorm web client

Jorm is a multi user dungeon (MUD) focused on grinding monsters in turn based combat, exploiting the flee mechanics to your advantage, and integer overflowing your EXP.

The game currently has over 200 items, over 40 enemies, and a few minor quests, including one dungeon with a boss and unique item as a reward.

Feel free to contact me on Discord about any questions, or join our Discord server to contribute ideas, report bugs, or hang out.
